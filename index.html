Who Am I?

I am currently working on my own startups in the areas of real-estate property management and semantic search.

I've worked as a Research Developer between 2009-2015 at the Multilingual Systems group, Microsoft Research,Bangalore. I work in the broad areas of Information Extraction and Retrieval, Machine learning and NLP, collaborating closely with Raghavendra Udupa & Santosh Vysyaraju.

Before joining Microsoft Research, I was at Yahoo Labs, Bangalore as an Intern. I was working on the automatic and efficient discovery of security vulnerabilities in the Yahoo codebase

Prior to Yahoo Research, I was pursuing B.Tech in Civil Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology,Bombay.
